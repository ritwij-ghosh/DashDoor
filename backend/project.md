2. Healthy Autopilot

An AI system that decides what you should eat before you get hungry, based on your calendar, travel schedule, and location.

Core idea:
It removes decision fatigue by pushing timely suggestions like:

“You have back-to-back interviews from 1 to 5. Order this now.”
“You land in Denver at 8:40 PM, these 3 nearby places fit your goals.”

MVP:

Connect Google Calendar
Add location/travel input manually
Recommend meals/snacks for next 8 to 12 hours
One tap order links or saved meal templates

Why it stands out:
Most nutrition apps react after the fact. This one proactively plans around chaos.
